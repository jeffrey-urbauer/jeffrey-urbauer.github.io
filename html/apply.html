Available Positions

Listed below are the various options for pursuing research in the Urbauer lab. In all cases, please contact Dr. Urbauer directly by email (urbauer@chem.uga.edu) to set up an appointment.

Postdoctoral Positions

Postdoctoral positions are available in the areas of protein biochemistry, molecular biology and biomolecular NMR spectroscopy.

Technical Positions

We are currently accepting applications for a technician to perform general molecular biology and protein biochemistry studies.

Graduate Students

We are currently accepting graduate students seeking M.S./Ph.D. degrees in Chemistry or Biochemistry and Molecular Biology.
